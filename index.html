Recycle NYC

Definitions:

Diversion Rate:

percentage of total municipal solid waste collected by the Department of Sanitation that is disposed of by recycling

Capture Rate:

percentage of total Paper or Metal/Glass/Plastic in the waste stream that is disposed of by recycling
